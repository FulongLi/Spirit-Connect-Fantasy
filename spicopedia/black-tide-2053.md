    <h1>Black Tide (2053)</h1>
    <p class="lead">
      "Black Tide" is the common name for the global conflict of 2053 — the war that broke humanity's
      faith in material civilization and redirected an entire century toward the inner world: spirit,
      consciousness, dream, and digital continuity.
    </p>
  </div>
</header>

<section class="section">
  <div class="container">
    <h2>Overview</h2>
    <p class="lead">
      Historians still debate what triggered the escalation of 2053. What is not debated is its aftermath:
      cities rebuilt themselves around consciousness industries rather than consumption, spiritual research
      received state funding for the first time in modern history, and the question "what survives us?"
      replaced "what do we own?" as the question of the age.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <div class="container">
    <h2>Legacy</h2>
    <p class="lead">
      The post-Black-Tide reconstruction created the regulatory and cultural environment in which
      consciousness-technology companies — including Spirit Connect — could exist at all. Some researchers
      note that several core technologies released in 2047, six years before the war, have never been fully
      explained by the public research record.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>
