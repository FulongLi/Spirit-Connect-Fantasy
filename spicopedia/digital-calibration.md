    <h1>Digital Calibration</h1>
    <p class="lead">
      Digital calibration is the scheduled procedure in which a person's consciousness record and their
      digital twin are synchronized. Under current IHinDiA guidelines it is performed at fixed age nodes,
      with the second calibration at age eighteen.
    </p>
  </div>
</header>

<section class="section">
  <div class="container">
    <h2>Overview</h2>
    <p class="lead">
      A calibration session is performed in a certified connection cabin under continuous monitoring.
      The procedure is painless, and the vast majority of sessions complete without incident. Participants
      describe the experience as a long, structured dream.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <div class="container">
    <h2>The 2118 anomaly</h2>
    <p class="lead">
      In 2118, a second-stage calibration session at a Spirit Connect facility ended in an anomaly involving
      multiple participants. The company's official statement describes a "non-standard connection event,
      fully contained." Independent verification of this statement is not available. This entry will be
      updated if that changes.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>
